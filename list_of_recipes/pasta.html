Mystical Pasta

Ingredients:

To make this recipe and please the all-father you will need:

  • 200gr Tagliatelle
  • 80gr Bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 100gr Pecorino Romano shredded
  • Pepper

Methodology:

First, ask the all-father for his blessing, then do these in order:

  1. Check the pasta packet for optimal cooking time
  2. Start boiling the pasta in salted water.
  3. Cut the bacon in cubes
  4. In a bowl mix the eggs with the cheese and add pepper liberarly
  5. When the pasta are ready, strain and keep the starch water
  6. Start frying the bacon in the pot
  7. Once it browns, add the pasta back into the pot
  8. Add a splash of the starch water and the egg-cheese mixure
  9. Stir rigorously, streaming water in as needed
  10. Once the mixture has a sauce consistency, transfer to a plate

Make an offering:

Humbly place the dish on the all-father's altar.

Take two steps back, offer the war prayer of your choosing

Wait for Valkyries to take you to Valhalla.
