Steak of the Gods

Ingredients:

To make this recipe and please the all-father you will need:

  • 400gr Beef Steak
  • 10ml Olive Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 10gr Butter

Methodology:

First, ask the all-father for his blessing, then do these in order:

  1. Cut the Garlic in thin slices
  2. Heat the pan till screaming hot
  3. Oil the pan
  4. Place the steak in the pan
  5. Fry for 4 minutes and then turn
  6. Place the butter and the garlic slices on the steak
  7. Let the butter melt on top of the garlic
  8. Baste the steak with the melted butter
  9. Remove from pan and let the steak rest for 5-10 minutes

Make an offering:

Humbly place the dish on the all-father's altar.

Take two steps back, offer the war prayer of your choosing

Wait for Valkyries to take you to Valhalla.
