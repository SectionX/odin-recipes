Ancient Salad

Ingredients:

To make this recipe and please the all-father you will need:

  • 150gr deboned chicken thighs
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 100gr of bacon
  • 200gr of lettuce
  • Mayonaise
  • Dijon Mustard
  • 10gr butter
  • 1 clove garlic

Methodology:

First, ask the all-father for his blessing, then do these in order:

  1. Remove the chicken skins in a cold pan and start rendering the fat
  2. Thinly slice the garlic and cut the bread in cubes
  3. Once enough fat has rendered to cover the pan, dispose of the skins
  4. Remove pan from heat, add bacon, butter and garlic in the pan and let it infuse
  5. Slice lettuce and place in a bowl
  6. Once bacon has browned, turn heat up, add the cubed bread and sautee till crispy
  7. Remove the croutons and start frying the chicken thighs whole, 4 minutes on each side
  8. Check for doneness and remove from heat. Cut the chicken in batons
  9. Add the chicken to the bowl, do not add the croutons yet
  10. Mix 2 tablespoons for mayonaise and 1 teaspoon of mustard, add extra mustard to taste
  11. Add the mixture to the bowl along with the croutons and bacon, then toss

Make an offering:

Humbly place the dish on the all-father's altar.

Take two steps back, offer the war prayer of your choosing

Wait for Valkyries to take you to Valhalla.
